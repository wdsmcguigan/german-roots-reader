# Skill: Pedagogical Critique for German Roots Reader

## What This Skill Is For

Use when evaluating whether the book's structure, chapter sequence, and content depth serve its intended readers — both German learners at various levels and language enthusiasts with no learning intent.

## Target Reader Profiles

**Profile A — The Active Learner**
- English speaker enrolled in a structured German course (Goethe Institut, TELC, university, DuoLingo)
- Roughly A1–B1 level; some vocabulary exposure, basic grammar awareness
- Uses this book alongside their course to understand the *why* behind what they're studying
- May jump to chapters relevant to what they're currently covering, rather than reading cover-to-cover

**Profile B — The Language Enthusiast**
- Curious about etymology, language history, or English word origins
- No active German learning; not planning to study German formally
- Reads cover-to-cover for pleasure and intellectual curiosity
- Easily lost if content assumes German study context

## Questions to Apply

**Scope and Sequence:**
- Does the chapter ordering make sense for both profiles? Does it build progressively for the learner while remaining coherent for the enthusiast reading straight through?
- Are appendices integrated into the reading flow, or are they isolated reference dumps?
- Does the book tell you how to use it — when to read which chapter, how it fits alongside formal study?

**Calibration:**
- Does each chapter assume the right level of prior exposure? Flag anything that would confuse a true beginner (Profile A at A0) or bore someone already past A2
- Does the book work without any German knowledge, or does it require some?
- Are German words and phrases adequately explained in context, or does explanation get assumed?

**Coverage:**
- Are there major topics that traditional courses cover (cases, verb conjugation, gender) that the book addresses? If it skips them, is that intentional and explained?
- Does Chapter 12 (synthesis) feel like a genuine culmination, or an arbitrary endpoint?

**Parallel-Course Use:**
- Can someone use this chapter-by-chapter alongside a Goethe/TELC curriculum? Does the chapter sequence roughly mirror how those courses introduce grammar and vocabulary?
